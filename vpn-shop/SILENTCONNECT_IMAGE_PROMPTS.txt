SilentConnect image prompts for Nano Banana Pro
Generated for Telegram bot assets.

General concept:
- SilentConnect should feel like a calm premium connectivity service, not a hacker/security product.
- The visual metaphor is "quiet private route": clean nodes, one protected route line, stable signal, soft product UI surfaces.
- Keep one recognizable brand motif across all images: two or three node points connected by a smooth S-shaped route line, with a small electric-cyan pulse/accent.
- Use the same palette everywhere: deep graphite background, dark teal surfaces, clean white typography, small electric-cyan accents.
- The images should look polished, trustworthy, and friendly for non-technical Telegram users.
- Avoid hacker cliches, padlocks, shields, hooded people, Matrix code, flags, military visuals, animals, cheap cyberpunk, QR codes, and clutter.
- Keep pricing and legal details out of the images. Prices and conditions should stay in bot captions/buttons.
- Generate at the highest available resolution.
